eez_bufs_together_50km.shp contains 50 km buffers for each EEZ-sea region. There are two buffers for each EEZ-sea region: one 50 km "inner" buffer (inside the EEZ-sea region) and one 50 km "outer' buffer (outside the EEZ-sea region on the high seas). 

Variables:
-MarRegion: EEZ-sea region
-type: Indicates whether buffer is an inner buffer or an outer buffer
-geometry: Geometry column compatible with sf package. The shape of the buffer.
